Cliff Villa

Alfajiri Cliff Villa boasts impressive views of the Indian Ocean and offers the ultimate privacy from its elevated position on a coral cliff. Consisting of four spacious bedrooms, the villa is perfectly suited to honeymooners or groups of up to 9 guests. Unique features include the opulent Turret Suite which has been rated as one of the most romantic bedrooms in the world and an infinity pool that seamlessly merges with the ocean.

Garden Villa

Surrounded by lush tropical greenery and an expansive garden flowing onto an intimate beach setting, Alfajiri Garden Villa accommodates up to 10 guests. Featuring four luxurious en-suite bedrooms (each uniquely curated), a large verandah, an infinity pool and jacuzzi, this is the ideal option for couples or two smaller families traveling together.

explore

Beach Villa

Located on the shores of Diani Beach, Alfajirii Beach Villa brings you to the water’s edge in a few easy steps from your verandah. Offering the ultimate beachfront living, the three-bedroom villa lies obscured between lush palm trees, providing an extra touch of privacy without compromising your ocean views. The ideal stay for up to 6 guests - families, friends or couples.
